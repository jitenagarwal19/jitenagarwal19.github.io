Bye Bye 2016

January 1, 2017    2016 thoughts learnings

It’s 1st January 2016 I am waking up little late than my usual time and I certainly miss the sun rise(DAMN!). Trying to do some reflection and thinking should i be happy about my previous year or should i be excited about the opportunities 2017 will bring. 2016 was quite eventful for me I lived this year more lively and consciously.

While trying to remember my events from 2016 tears(yes I am man and I cry sometimes) are trickling down from my eyes may be there is something more i could have done maybe its the place where i am working now is not the right one for me and I expected myself to move out from here but couldn’t. Maybe in 2016 my startup should have took off but didn’t. I am looking in the mirror(also typing this blog at the same time) and trying not to get gloomy about things that I wasn’t able to achieve but I should be happy about the things that I learned and be grateful about another happy year of my life.

The real question that I should be asking myself is are you a better version of yourself from what you were last year - And the answer comes yes,

I am obviuosly a much better person than I was when 2016 started, I was rude, arrogant, fat, not doing much learning, not travelling much, not talking to people much.

I took bad decisions which led me to failures that will always happen every year,

Every year there will be - 
    New sets of failures,
    New sets of success, 
    Bad decisions,
    People that i might loose contact from,
    Happy moments which I wished would have never ended,
    Events I wished would never have crossed paths with,
    Learnings that will remain engraved with me for the rest of my life,
    Sights that my eyes will see and i will never forget, 
    Words that came out of my mouth that would make or break someoneâ€™s heart. 

Every year i will be growing....
